Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services? Find answers to commonly asked questions below.

1. What is Permanent Staffing?

Permanent staffing refers to hiring employees on a long-term basis to fill permanent positions within an organization.

2. How does Temporary Staffing work?

Temporary staffing involves hiring employees for short-term assignments or projects, typically to address temporary workload fluctuations or specific project needs.

3. What services do your Payroll Services include?

Our payroll services include accurate payroll processing, tax compliance, dedicated support, customized solutions, and technology integration.

4. Why is Background Verification important?

Background verification is important to ensure the reliability of potential hires and mitigate risks associated with hiring discrepancies, such as criminal records or falsified credentials.
